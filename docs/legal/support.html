Need help with an order?
Email support@lexmakesit.com and include your order number (shown on the order status screen). We aim to reply within a few hours during business hours.

Frequently asked questions

How do I place an order?

Browse restaurants on the home screen (or tap "Spin the Wheel" if you can't decide), add items to your cart, and check out. You'll get a live status screen that updates as the restaurant accepts and the driver delivers.

Why is the app asking for my location?

Location is used only to show nearby restaurants and estimate delivery distance and time. You can decline and still order normally.

My order was declined — what happens?

If a restaurant can't take your order, you'll see a "declined" message with the reason, and you won't be charged. Just pick another spot.

How do refunds work?

If something's wrong with your order, email us with your order number and we'll make it right.

I own a restaurant — how do I join?

We'd love to add you. Email support@lexmakesit.com with your restaurant name and location.
