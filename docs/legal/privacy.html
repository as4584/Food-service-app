NJ Eats Privacy Policy

North Jersey Eats — local food ordering & delivery

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This policy explains what information the NJ Eats mobile app collects, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We keep it short and plain because that is how we run the business.

Information we collect

  • Order information — the name you provide, your delivery address, and the items you order.
  • Location — with your permission, your device's location is used to show nearby restaurants and estimate delivery distance and time. You can decline location access and still browse and order; you just won't see distance-based sorting.
  • Device & usage data — basic technical information (app version, error reports) needed to keep the app working reliably.

How we use it

  • To take, prepare, and deliver your order.
  • To show you relevant nearby restaurants and accurate delivery estimates.
  • To provide customer support and improve the app.

Payments

When card payments are enabled, they are processed by a PCI-compliant payment provider (such as Stripe). NJ Eats does not store your full card number on our own servers.

Who we share it with

We share only what is necessary to fulfill your order: the restaurant you ordered from receives your order details, and the delivery driver assigned to your order receives your delivery address. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep order records for as long as needed to operate the service, resolve disputes, and meet legal and tax obligations. You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Your choices

  • You can turn location access on or off at any time in your device settings.
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your data by emailing us.

Children

NJ Eats is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@lexmakesit.com.

NJ Eats is operated by LexMakesIt. This page is provided as the app's privacy policy for the Apple App Store and Google Play.
